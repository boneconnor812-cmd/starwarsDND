Ace Pilot

"I can fly anything." Starfighter legends, daredevil scouts, and navigators who view asteroid fields as casual obstacle courses.

5e Engine Chassis: Ranger
Recommended Subclass: Gloom Stalker (Combat Flight Specialist)
Special Trait: Subclass tracking, darkvision, and speed rules natively apply to vehicles they operate.
