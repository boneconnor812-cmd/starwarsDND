Jedi Consular (Mystic / Seer)

"To seek knowledge is to understand the Force." Sages, diplomats, and seers who solve galactic crises through insight, mediation, and deep mental masteries.

5e Engine Chassis: Wizard
Recommended Subclass: School of Divination (Way of Foresight)
Core Concept Substitution: Spellbook = Jedi Holocron Journal (Stores your mastered cosmic archives)

Class Features & Progression

Level Feature / Power Unlocked Description & Reflavoring
1Force Initiate / Holocron MechanicsLearn Force techniques exactly like Wizard spells. Relies on Intelligence.
2Force Visions (Portent)Roll two d20s after a long rest. Swap any visible creature's d20 roll with your stored results.
3-5Force Scholar (3rd-Level Powers)Unlock Telekinetic Lifts, Advanced Mind Tricks, and Disrupting Force Flows (Counterspell).
6Enhanced Force InsightWhen casting a Divination power, recover a lower-level spell slot as the Force guides your efficiency.
7-9Jedi Sage (5th-Level Powers)Master Advanced Force Manipulation (Telekinesis), Farseeing, and Force Barriers.
10Expanded Force SightGain permanent access to Force Perception, Darkvision, or Force Truth following a meditation rest.
11-13Council Master (7th-Level Powers)Cast Force Cage (Force Prisons) and Journey Through the Cosmic Force (Plane Shift).
14Grand Force VisionYou now roll and store 3 Portent Dice instead of 2 after long rests.
15-17Grand Master (9th-Level Powers)Perfect Precognition (Foresight) and Legendary Force Miracles (Wish).
18Force MasteryChoose one 1st and one 2nd level power to cast completely at-will without spending energy.
20Grand Master of the OrderChoose two 3rd-level techniques. They are always prepared and castable for free once per short rest.

Recommended Force Powers

  • Cantrips: Mage Hand (Minor Telekinesis), Mind Sliver (Mental Disturbance).
  • 1st Level: Shield (Force Barrier), Charm Person (Jedi Mind Trick), Sleep (Calming Force Wave).
  • 2nd Level: Hold Person (Force Stasis), Suggestion (Advanced Mind Trick).
