Mandalorian (Armored Warrior)

"This is the Way." Walking arsenals protected by customized Beskar armor plates, jetpacks, and a strict warrior creed.

5e Engine Chassis: Artificer
Recommended Subclass: Armorer (Mandalorian Blacksmith Forge)
Signature Arsenal: Shock Gauntlets (Thunder Gauntlets), Integrated Wrist Blasters (Lightning Launcher).
