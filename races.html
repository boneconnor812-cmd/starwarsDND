Galactic Species

Reskinning official 5e Player Handbook rules to bring iconic Star Wars lineages into your campaign without homebrew headache.

Human

"Humans are found in every corner of the galaxy, driving the expansion of both ancient republics and aggressive empires alike."

5e Mechanics Base Traits & Features (Star Wars Flavor)
Variant Human (PHB)
  • Ability Scores: +1 to any two attributes of choice.
  • Versatility: Gain proficiency in 1 skill of your choice.
  • Core Feat: Gain 1 bonus feat at Level 1 (e.g., Sharpshooter for Commandos or Telekinetic for Force Adepts).

Twi'lek

"Elegant, perceptive, and highly adaptable, Twi'leks navigate the dangerous social landscapes of the Outer Rim and criminal underworld with ease."

5e Mechanics Base Traits & Features (Star Wars Flavor)
Tiefling (PHB)
  • Ability Scores: +2 Charisma, +1 Intelligence.
  • Lekku Awareness (Darkvision): See in dim light up to 60 feet.
  • Environmental Adaptation (Hellish Resistance): Resistance to Fire damage (reflavored as extreme thermal tolerance to hot desert climates like Ryloth).
  • Force Allure (Thaumaturgy/Legacy Spells): Access to basic mind-soothing cantrips or minor presence control.

Wookiee

"Massive, fiercely loyal, and terrifying when provoked. A Wookiee's roar can break a mercenary's morale before a single shot is fired."

5e Mechanics Base Traits & Features (Star Wars Flavor)
Half-Orc (PHB)
  • Ability Scores: +2 Strength, +1 Constitution.
  • Intimidating Presence (Menacing): Skill proficiency in Intimidation.
  • Wookiee Rage (Relentless Endurance): When dropped to 0 HP, drop to 1 HP instead once per long rest.
  • Savage Strikes (Savage Attacks): Roll an extra weapon damage die on a critical hit with a physical/vibro weapon.

Chiss

"Hailing from the Unknown Regions, the blue-skinned Chiss are renowned for their highly disciplined military minds, tactical genius, and cool demeanor."

5e Mechanics Base Traits & Features (Star Wars Flavor)
High Elf (PHB)
  • Ability Scores: +2 Dexterity, +1 Intelligence.
  • Infrared Sight (Darkvision): See up to 60 feet clearly in complete darkness.
  • Calculated Mind (Fey Ancestry): Advantage on saving throws against being charmed or mentally manipulated.
  • Tactical Study (Trance): Instead of sleeping, enter a 4-hour hyper-focused data-review and meditative cycle.
  • Basic Training: Learn 1 tactical/utility engineering cantrip or technology trick.

Zabrak

"Recognizable by their vestigial facial horns and striking tattoos, Zabyrak possess an ironclad resilience and stubborn independence."

5e Mechanics Base Traits & Features (Star Wars Flavor)
Mountain Dwarf (PHB)
  • Ability Scores: +2 Strength, +2 Constitution (representing elite physical hardening).
  • Species Resilience (Dwarven Resilience): Advantage on saving throws against poison and resistance to poison damage.
  • Survival Instincts: Proficiency with light and medium combat armor out-of-the-gate.

Mon Calamari

"Amphibious beings from an oceanic world, Mon Calamari are naturally brilliant engineers, high-level tactical officers, and ideal starship pilots."

5e Mechanics Base Traits & Features (Star Wars Flavor)
Triton (Volo's / MMoM)
  • Ability Scores: +1 Strength, +1 Constitution, +1 Charisma (or standard floating +2/+1).
  • Amphibious: Can breathe both air and water seamlessly.
  • Aquatic Agility: Base walking speed of 30 feet and a swimming speed of 30 feet.
  • Deep Diver (Guardians of the Depths): Resistance to cold damage and adapted to survive high-pressure deep-sea environments.

Droid (Synthetic Chassis)

"Whether a custom-built tech specialist companion or an independent mechanical actor, droids offer unparalleled processing and resilience."

5e Mechanics Base Traits & Features (Star Wars Flavor)
Warforged (Eberron)
  • Ability Scores: +2 Constitution, +1 to one other attribute of choice.
  • Constructed Resilience: Immune to disease; advantage on saving throws against being poisoned; doesn't need to eat, drink, or breathe.
  • Recharge Mode (Sentry's Rest): Instead of sleeping, go into a low-power diagnostics standby mode for 6 hours. Remain fully conscious of surroundings.
  • Integrated Plating: +1 bonus to Armor Class (AC) representing defensive composite alloy plating.
  • Specialized Subroutines: Gain 1 skill proficiency and 1 tool specialization of your choice.
