Smuggler (Scoundrel)

"Never tell me the odds." High-stakes gamblers, quick-draw gunslingers, and underworld merchants who run the hyperlanes.

5e Engine Chassis: Rogue
Recommended Subclass: Swashbuckler (Gunslinger Duelist)
Combat Reflavor: Sneak Attack = Dirty Fighting / Well-placed critical blaster shots.

Class Features & Progression

Level Feature / Power Unlocked Description & Reflavoring
1Dirty Fighting / Underworld CantDeal extra Sneak Attack damage to vulnerable targets. Know code scripts and smuggler networks.
2Cunning Action MatrixDash, Disengage, or Hide using a simple Bonus Action.
3Quick Draw / Smuggler's LuckFancy Footwork: Hit a target and walk away safely without drawing attacks of opportunity. Add Charisma to Initiative.
5-7Uncanny Dodge & EvasionUse reactions to halve incoming blaster damage. Completely bypass explosive area hazards.
9Panache (Fast Talker)Taunt hostile enemies or charm neutral targets using pure witty charisma checks.
11Reliable Talent IntegrationAny proficient skill check rolling below a 9 natively counts as a 10. You don't make amateur mistakes.
13Elegant ManeuverBonus Action: Instantly grant yourself advantage on Acrobatics or Athletics checks to swing from cranes or slide out of handcuffs.
15Slippery Mind MatrixGain permanent proficiency in Wisdom saving throws; mind tricks are highly ineffective on you.
17Legendary GunslingerMaster Duelist: Miss a shot? Spend a reaction to completely reroll the attack with advantage once per short rest.
20Stroke of LuckTurn any missed attack into an automatic hit or any failed skill check into a Natural 20 once per short rest.
