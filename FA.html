Force Adept (Grey / Unaligned)

"I don't follow the Jedi Order, nor the Sith code. I follow the Force itself." Untamed, emotional, and raw conduits of cosmic power.

5e Engine Chassis: Sorcerer
Force Alignment Choice: Light Adept (Divine Soul) vs Dark Adept (Shadow Sorcery) vs Grey.
Core Mechanic: Force Manipulation (Metamagic). Bend paths of powers through Subtle alignment or Twinned targeting matrices.

Class Features & Progression
