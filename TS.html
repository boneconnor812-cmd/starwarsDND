Tech Specialist (Engineer / Slicer)

"I can slice this console, override the security doors, and repair our ship's shields at the same time." Masterminds of technology and droid programming.

5e Engine Chassis: Artificer
Recommended Subclass: Battle Smith (Droid Master)
Core Trait: Combat Technician. Natively use your Intelligence modifier instead of Strength/Dexterity for all blaster and weapon attacks.
