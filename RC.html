Republic Commando (Soldier)

"Form up, squad! Take down those targets!" Frontline operators, ARC Troopers, and heavy weapons specialists bred for impossible missions.

5e Engine Chassis: Fighter
Recommended Subclass: Battle Master (Tactical Specialist)
Standard Gear Reskin: DC-17 Blaster Rifle (Heavy Crossbow stats), Vibroknife (Shortsword stats), Combat Armor.

Class Features & Progression

Tactical Orders (Maneuvers)

  • Precision Attack: Steady your scope to add your superiority die directly to an attack roll.
  • Menacing Attack: Lay down heavy suppressive fire, forcing the target to become frightened of you.
  • Rally: Bark a battlefield cry that bolsters a teammate with temporary hit points.
