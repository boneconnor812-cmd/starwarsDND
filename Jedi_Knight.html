Jedi Knight (Guardian)

"The Force is my ally, and a powerful ally it is." Defenders of peace, justice, and the frontline protectors of the Galactic Republic.

5e Engine Chassis: Paladin
Recommended Subclass: Oath of the Ancients (Reflavored as Oath of the Light / Jedi Code)
Core Weapon Substitution: Longsword = Lightsaber (Deals radiant damage instead of slashing)

Class Features & Progression

Level Feature / Power Unlocked Description & Reflavoring
1Jedi Initiate / Force Sense / Lay on HandsDetect Light/Dark side presence within 60ft. Force Healing pool (5 HP per level).
2Fighting Style & Force StrikeChoose Defense, Dueling, or Protection. Expend Force power slots to deal extra radiant lightsaber damage.
3Force Purity & Jedi Code (Oath)Immune to disease. Access to Force Bind (restrain target) and Rebuke the Dark (repel dark spirits).
4ASI / Feat UpgradeBoost stats or pick feats like Defensive Duelist or War Caster.
5Extra AttackGain 2 lightsaber attacks per action. Unlocks 2nd-level Force powers (Force Healing, Find Speeder).
6Aura of ResolveAllies within 10 feet add your Charisma modifier to all saving throws.
7Aura of LightYou and nearby allies gain resistance to spell, Force, and energy damage.
8-10Aura of BraveryImmune to being frightened. Access to 3rd-level spells (Blinding Saber Flourish, Sustained Healing Field).
11Improved Force StrikeEvery melee strike natively deals an extra 1d8 radiant damage as your saber hums with the Force.
12-14Force ProtectionAccess to 4th-level spells like Death Ward (Force protection vs lethal blows).
15Unyielding JediIf reduced to 0 HP, drop to 1 HP instead through intense Force focus.
17-19Grand AuraAll protective combat auras permanently expand from 10 feet to a 30-foot radius.
20Avatar of the ForceAssume a glowing, radiant form for 1 minute: Regain 10 HP per turn, enemies have disadvantage vs your powers.

Key Reskinned Force Powers (Spells)

  • 1st Level: Bless (Battle Meditation), Shield of Faith (Force Barrier), Heroism (Inspiring Aura).
  • 3rd Level: Blinding Smite (Brilliant Saber Strike), Revivify (Force Resuscitation).
  • 5th Level: Circle of Power (Massive Protective Meditation Field).
